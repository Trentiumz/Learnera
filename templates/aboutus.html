About Us

Hello! This project was developed by Daniel Y, Kevin Z, Pradyumn J, and Lucas K, and we are high school students graduating in '24. We chose to program Studyera because we wanted to provide resources for efficient learning of educational materials.
During this COVID-19 pandemic we witnessed teachers trying to teach in an interactive yet informative manner. However we noticed that such resources were not often accessible to all, with many requiring payment subscriptions. That led us to the creation of Studyera. Our goals with Studyera are to provide a platform where learners and educators can feel free to pursue their joy for education while having access to resources for success.
