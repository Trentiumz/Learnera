Instructions

Hello! Welcome to Studyera! Studyera is a classroom-assist tool focused on helping students learn through quizzing. On this platform users can create lessons using our wide array of tools. Anyone can create a lesson and add questions to the lesson. Lessons are public and can be viewed by anyone, however only the owner can edit their own lessons. There are 4 tools available for creating lessons, these include terms or definitions, fill in the blanks, multiple choice, and math format. Upon clicking 'Access Tools' you will be able to select which tool you would like to use for your purposes.
On the home page you can click login to view your profile. There is a search bar at the top to discover lessons and courses. After logging in, you can go into your account and see your lessons and courses.
